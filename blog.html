Inferno
Destroy two critical gas lines.

Description

Inferno is one of the top maps in the competetive map pool. It consists of three main lanes which are middle, bannanna, and middle apartments. Middle connects to bannanna through arches and connects to mid apartments through long hall. The map takes place in an unknown village and it has counter terrorists defending two critical gas lines from the terrorists.
